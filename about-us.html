  • About Us

Your goals. Our priority.

At Merican Bank, we’re a direct reflection of our customers. In fact, it’s their goals and aspirations that define every aspect of our bank – from the products and services we create to the personalized customer care we provide.

Since our start in Corpus Christi in 1970, we’ve guided individuals into early retirement, provided financial solutions for generations of families and helped small business owners become bigger business owners. Over the years we, too, have become pretty big. But we proudly retain our independent, community bank spirit.

Today, we serve the Greater Corpus Christi area as well as Greater Austin and the Victoria/Goliad markets. Customers also have convenient access to a number of bank-owned ATM locations as well as thousands of ATM network locations worldwide.

Step through our doors and experience banking the way it should be – personalized and always committed to your financial success. Step back outside and experience a refreshing newfound confidence in the future of you, your family, and your business.

How many banks truly offer this? We can count them on one finger.
